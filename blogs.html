Mid-Journey The best AI image generator

Midjourney is a generative artificial intelligence program and service created by a San Francisco-based independent research lab. Led by David Holz, co-founder of Leap Motion, Midjourney creates images from natural language descriptions, similar to OpenAI's DALL-E and Stable Diffusion. It is currently in open beta and has been releasing new model versions regularly. Users can access Midjourney through a Discord bot and use prompts to generate images.

Will AI take jobs in IT field?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is undoubtedly transforming the job market, including the IT field. While it is predicted that AI will replace some jobs, it will also create new opportunities. The World Economic Forum estimates that AI will replace 85 million jobs by 2025 but also create 97 million new roles.Jobs requiring critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity are less likely to be automated and may even be enhanced by AI.
